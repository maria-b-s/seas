Request a standard or enhanced DBS check

This service is for Registered Bodies, not individuals.

If you're a countersignatory, you can use this service to:

  1. request a standard or enhanced DBS check for employees or volunteers
  2. send an online application form to the applicant
  3. download a copy of the applicant's answers to send to an ID checker
  4. track the progress of all your applications

What you'll need

To request a standard or enhanced DBS check you'll need your:

  • Registered Body number
  • countersignatory number

You must also have details of the applicant. You need their:

  • email address
  • first and last name
  • job title or role
  • employer or organisation name
