Request a standard or enhanced DBS check

Apply for a Standard or Enhanced Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check to get a copy of a job applicant or employee's criminal record. It’s available for people working in England and Wales.

You can also get a standard or enhanced DBS check for someone who lives in Northern Ireland or Scotland but the job they’re applying for is in England or Wales. There’s a different way to apply if the job is in Northern Ireland or Scotland.

If you're an employer requesting a standard or enhanced check for the first time, you'll need to create a DBS account.
