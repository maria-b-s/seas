Request a standard or enhanced DBS check

If you're a registered body, you can use this service to:

  1. Request a standard or enhanced DBS check for a new employee, post holder or volunteer.
  2. Send an online application form to the applicant.
  3. Check the applicant's details.
  4. Submit the application to DBS.

What you'll need

To request a standard or enhanced DBS check you'll need:

  • your registered body number
  • your countersignatory number
  • the job or role the check is for
  • the name of the employer or organisation
  • the name and email address of the applicant
