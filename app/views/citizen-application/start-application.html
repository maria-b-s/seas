Complete a requested standard or enhanced DBS check

Use this service to complete your standard or enhanced DBS check application. You must be 16 years or older to use this service.

What you'll need

Before you start make sure you have:

  • your addresses for the last 5 years and the dates you lived there
  • details of any other names you've used and the dates you used them
  • a current address we can post your DBS certificate to

If you have them, you will also need your:

  • passport, this can be a UK or non-UK passport
  • National Insurance number
  • UK driving licence

You’ll need to sign into GOV.UK One Login to use this service. If you do not have a GOV.UK One Login account, you can create one.
